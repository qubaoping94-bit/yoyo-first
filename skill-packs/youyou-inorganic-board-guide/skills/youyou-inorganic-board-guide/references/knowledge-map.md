# Youyou Inorganic Board Knowledge Map

Use this file for product explanations, customer/dealer education, and safe claim boundaries.

## Core Positioning

- Youyou inorganic board should be introduced as a material system, not a single generic panel.
- Core thesis: `把无机板，做成整装材料系统。`
- Customer-facing summary: Youyou uses an inorganic-board base to extend into doors, walls, furniture boards, and whole-space applications, so customers can understand it as a door-wall-cabinet integrated inorganic-space solution.
- Stable long-form expression: `从一张无机板，到一个高端空间系统。`

## Product System

- **优优无机板**: the core board material and the base layer for the broader product system.
- **优优无机门**: door panel uses 5 mm Youyou inorganic board as the surface panel, with a quiet mechanical core material.
- **优优无机墙板**: multi-layer solid-wood composite `2 + 8 + 2` structure, total thickness 12 mm.
- **优优无机复合家具板**: solid-wood composite `2 + 14 + 2` structure, total thickness 18 mm.
- **门墙柜一体化无机空间**: the recommended strategic framing when customers ask why the product matters beyond a single board.

## Material Story

- Use stable material language: `玄武岩体系`, `高温熔融`, `拉丝成纤`, `材料稳定`, `复合增强潜力`.
- Avoid unverified numerical claims about fire rating, antibacterial rate, waterproof duration, deformation rate, service life, patent count, or national standards unless the current source material explicitly provides them.
- When customers ask for proof, recommend viewing the public product-evolution demo pages first, then asking for official reports or project documents for final technical confirmation.

## Customer Value

- Explain value through the path `材料逻辑 -> 结构体系 -> 产品形态 -> 空间应用 -> 工程交付信心`.
- Emphasize that Youyou is not positioned as a normal catalog-style building material page; it focuses on product system, structural proof, use scenarios, and confidence in delivery.
- Strong customer phrasing:
  - `不是只卖一张板，而是把无机板延展成门、墙、柜和整装空间。`
  - `先看材料逻辑，再看产品结构，最后看它能落到哪些空间。`
  - `客户如果只问单项性能，建议直接给对应专项演示页。`

## Application Scenarios

- commercial spaces
- hotels
- education
- healthcare
- office projects
- transportation or public spaces
- dealer showroom and project presentation scenarios

## Dealer Communication

- Dealers usually need a simple viewing route, not a dense technical lecture.
- Recommended route:
  1. Send the website hub so the customer sees the complete public-material system.
  2. Send the product-evolution hub or one relevant demo page for the customer's pain point.
  3. Use the gallery and moments copy site for follow-up visuals and shareable copy.
- When the customer is early-stage, lead with `整体认知`.
- When the customer has a specific objection, lead with the matching专项演示 page.
- When the customer needs to forward internally, provide a short WeChat-style message plus one link.

## Claim Boundaries

- Do not claim a fixed price without quotation conditions.
- Do not claim exact construction delivery terms without a project plan.
- Do not claim specific testing standards or values unless the source is present.
- Do not compare by disparaging competitors. Prefer `适合关注...的客户` and `可以重点看...`.
- If asked for final engineering decisions, say the product information is for early understanding and final selection should combine samples, drawings, reports, and project requirements.
